FUCK SHIRLEY is TURNING FIFTY


I’ve still got it. Right? A lot has happened in 50 years.

I celebrate my 50th birthday in an unexpected and crazy times in 2020, in true quarantine fashion and surrounding myself with people who truly are very special. In many ways, it is a time of gratitude and appreciation. Friendships over the years have and continue to be cherished. I am blessed with amazing and close family and friends--I am beyond grateful.

My son and daughter have been my greatest gifts in this lifetime.

I have taken risks, dreamed BIG, and tried to be different, I have succeeded and have failed as well. My mistakes, challenge, and obstacles have been my greatest teachers. I have learned that progression is where its AT!

In this life, I have been criticized, hurt, and have been treated poorly. I also have been appreciated, recognized, admired, loved, and treated incredibly well. I have loved and have been in love--I have known unconditional love, both giving and receiving it. I have experienced both great joy, great loss, sadness, and sorrow.

To keep evolving is a given for me. Another really dope verison of me is emerging. I have and continue to have passion and conviction, determination, and influence. I have been intimidated and scared and in a position to pray for courage, strength, and wisdom.

It is about being age positive and loving the life I already live, the woman I am and the skin I'm in.

I believe feelings that start with a small flame inside and presents themselves as you face the world and say “This is me... LIKE it or NOT. In many ways, fifty feels unashamedly about SELF.

Self-acceptance, self-awareness and self-care too. I plan on practicing all of these without guilt, regret or a moment more of hesitation.

Welcome Fifty!!
