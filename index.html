FUCK SHIRLEY is TURNING FIFTY


I’ve still got it. Right? A lot has happened in 50 years.

I celebrated my 50th birthday in true quarantine fashion and surrounding myself with people who truly are very special. In many ways, it is a time of gratitude and appreciation. Friendships over the years have and continue to be cherished. I am blessed with amazing and close friends--I am grateful.

My children have been my greatest gifts and my two amazing grandkids.

I have dreamed, I have taken risks, I have dared to be different, I have succeeded and have failed as well. My mistakes, challenge, and obstacles have been my greatest teachers. I have learned that perfection sucks and is a myth, progress is where it is at! In this life, I have been criticized, hurt, and have been treated poorly at times. I also have been appreciated, recognized, admired, loved, and treated incredibly well. I have loved and have been in love--I have known unconditional love, both giving it and receiving it. I have experienced great joy and I have experienced great loss, sadness, and sorrow.

To keep evolving is a given for me. Another really dope verison of me is emerging. I have had such passion and conviction, determination, and influence. I have also been intimidated and scared and in a position to pray for courage, strength, and wisdom.

It is about being age positive and loving the life I already live, the woman I am and the skin I'm in.

I believe feelings like these start with a small fire inside and show themselves as the face you turn to the world that says “This is me... LIKE it or NOT. Do look for the things that warrant a second glance, raise a smile or make you lean in closer to hear what I’m saying.” In many ways, fifty feels unashamedly about SELF.

Self-acceptance, self-awareness and self-care too. I plan on practicing all of these without guilt, regret or a moment more of hesitation.
Welcome Fifty!!
